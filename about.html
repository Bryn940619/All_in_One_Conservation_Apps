About EcoGuard

EcoGuard is a cutting-edge wildlife conservation and monitoring platform built to support rangers, researchers, and local communities in safeguarding biodiversity. By seamlessly combining advanced mapping of wildlife sightings, real-time threat detection, and data-driven insights, EcoGuard harnesses technology to drive effective conservation efforts for a healthier, more resilient planet.

Our mission is to empower environmental stewardship through innovative tools, precise data, and collaborative action—fostering a global community united in protecting nature’s priceless heritage.
